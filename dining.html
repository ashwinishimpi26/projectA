Tasteful Interiors, Flavourful Food.

The taste takes on a whole new meaning at our award-winning restaurants and lounges – where bold flavors and dramatic design come together in grand fashion. Savor the most of every moment with our legendary and authentic global cuisine, imaginative menus, and uniquely immersive dining experience.

China House Restaurant

Treat your soul to Sichuanese delights, in the heart of Mumbai. The award-winning China House Restaurant is your gateway to a traditional experience of Sichuanese cuisine. Choose from sublime handmade dim sums, black pepper tofu/crab, Peking duck, Jasmine tea tiramisu, and more, while seated outdoors in our Pagoda setting, or indoors in family-dining style.

Celini

Experience the glorious flavors of Italian home-style cooking at Mumbai’s finest, award-winning Italian restaurant. Celini’s kitchen boasts a wood-fired oven, rotisserie, and charcoal grill, for a more delicate and flavorful experience – choose from our traditional pizzas, pastas, lasagna, and more, handcrafted with a modern twist.

Soma

Sample our authentic North Indian and North-West frontier cuisine, straight from the tandoor, at the award-winning Soma restaurant. Watch your food being prepared live – delectable kebab platters, luxurious creamy curries, delicately braised meats, and more, in colors and flavors as striking as our iconic Indian artwork.

Fifty Five East

Indulge in a fine feast of cuisines at Fifty Five East – our multi-cuisine buffet restaurant, offering Thai, Japanese, Lebanese mezze, Western and Indian specialties, prepared live. Or join us for the Grand Sunday Brunch, a glorious spread of the most spectacular signature dishes from our signature restaurants and delicatessen.

Lobby Lounge

Join in on the latest buzz over a cup of coffee, catch up with friends over a laidback meal, or work out your best-selling idea – do it all in grand style. Choose from our all-day dining menu, with Indian and international offerings, including afternoon high tea.

The Bar

Style and substance come together at The Bar to create a heady cocktail of alluring elegance and charismatic taste. Make your selection from our all-day dining menu, or our exhaustive drinks menu, featuring a range of vintage wines, whiskeys, and more. The hotel watering hole is centrally located, making it the perfect pre-meal meetup spot.

In-Room Dining
